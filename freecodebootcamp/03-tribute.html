Personality

Roman, nicknamed Romulus after the founder and first king of Rome, is a snarky, masochistic, and brash individual who thrives off of humiliation (both his own and others). In the early days of the show, Roman played the part of court jester, making crude remarks to his coworkers and subordinates, and belittling anyone beneath his station due to a deep insecurity brought on by his father's parental tactics. As the show progresses, so does Roman. He becomes more tactful, using the bullying and leverage learned from his father in order to secure major deals for the company, earning him a smidge of Logan's respect. Unfortunately, Roman is still incredibly immature, and cost himself the seat at his father's table after sending a lewd photo to one of his superiors.
